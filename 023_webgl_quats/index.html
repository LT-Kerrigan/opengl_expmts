Quaternion Rotation Demo

axis of rotation: x y z
angle: (degrees)

normalise(axis) = [0.0, 1.0, 0.0]

versor = [
  cos (angle),
  x * sin (0.5 * angle),
  y * sin (0.5 * angle),
  z * sin (0.5 * angle)
]
= [w, x, y, z]

mat4 R = quaternion_to_matrix (versor)
